About Us

About NMDC Group

NMDC Group is a prominent figure in the fields of engineering, marine dredging, procurement, and construction, working in the Middle East and further afield.

We specialize in providing innovative solutions for those vital sectors at the core of the UAE economy. This includes addressing the complex future challenges facing the energy EPC sector.

Under our umbrella, we own and operate four business units: NMDC Dredging & Marine, NMDC Energy (formerly NPCC), NMDC Engineering, and NMDC Construction (formerly EE). Committed to excellence and led by responsibility, we deliver projects of unrivalled quality, adhering to the most rigorous third party certifications. Together, we deliver smart, sustainable solutions tailored to the MENA region, shaping the future one exceptional project at a time.

Sustainable World

For NMDC Group, Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment (QHSE) is the cornerstone of our activities. We continuously take steps to minimise the environmental impact of our operations and protect the health and safety of our people and the public. Through transparency, integrity, and regular improvement, we strive to lead the way as a responsible corporate citizen, and uphold our Environment, Social, and Governance (ESG) objectives, as well as contributing to the decarbonization of the UAE.
